Quicker, more environmentally friendly deliveries

Dlvrry provides an on demand, last mile delivery service which helps the environment and provides a much better customer experience! It also creates new job oppurtunities for your local community so it's a win win all around!

We are about the environment and people

Dlvrry was started from a problem we seen when trying to run a small business. Delivery rates were high with long delivery times, deliveries to local postcodes would go through an unnecessary process where the parcel would travel further than it needed to go in turn, creating a unnecessary larger footprint. Our main focus was our customers, small businesses rely so much of their customers satisfaction. If a customer is not happy, they will move on. Dlvrry can help with that satisfaction by providing on demand, last mile delivery. Customers can order from you today and recieve their parcels in a matter of hours*.
