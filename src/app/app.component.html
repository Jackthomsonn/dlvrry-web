We are about the environment and people

Dlvrry was started from a problem we saw when trying to run a small business. Delivery rates were high with long delivery times, deliveries to local postcodes would go through an unnecessary process where the parcel would travel further than it needed to go which in turn creates an unnecessary larger footprint. Dlvrry can help with providing a delivery service that will boost customer satisfaction by offering on-demand, last-mile delivery. Customers can order from you today and receive their parcels in a matter of hours*.
